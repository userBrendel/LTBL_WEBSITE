LTBL TRAINING & DEVELOPMENT INSTITUTE

IDENTITY

Let There Be Light (LTBL) Training and Development Institute was established in the year 2019 with the vision of training children and adults alike for academic and skills excellence. As small as we are, we are sure of our legitimacy by having a KHDA-approved as well as achieved quality status with our certifying partner. Though the process was rigorous, we persevered believing that to see excellence, we should be excellent as well.
