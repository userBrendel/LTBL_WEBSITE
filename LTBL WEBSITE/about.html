ABOUT US

LTBL TRAINING & DEVELOPMENT INSTITUTE

MISSION

To provide quality training that empowers student’s competence while instilling the importance of moral values.

VISION

LTBL is an Institution producing future leaders with competence, commitment and character.

IDENTITY

Let There Be Light (LTBL) Training and Development Institute was established in the year 2019 with the vision of training children and adults alike for academic and skills excellence. As small as we are, we are sure of our legitimacy by having a KHDA-approved as well as achieved quality status with our certifying partner. Though the process was rigorous, we persevered believing that to see excellence, we should be excellent as well. Our humble beginning consisted of 25 trainees and 3 trainers. Amidst the small number, a healthy community was formed.
