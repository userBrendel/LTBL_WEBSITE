ZOO FIELD-TRIP

As they board the buses for the journey back to school, the students chatter excitedly, their conversations filled with tales of the day's adventures and plans to share their newfound knowledge with family and friends. For the students of Let There Be Light , the zoo school field trip isn't just an outing; it's an opportunity to broaden their horizons, and cultivate a lifelong love of learning.

2023 GRADUATION

At the Let There Be Light Institute's graduation ceremony, awards were not just tokens of achievement; they were symbolic acknowledgments of the students' dedication, character, and service within the community. Each award carried with it a narrative of perseverance, leadership, and impact, reflecting the values instilled in the students throughout their time at the institute.

ADVENTURE

One of the highlights of LTBL Adventure Day is the opportunity for students to step outside their comfort zones and try new things. Whether it's conquering a ropes course, navigating an obstacle course, or testing their balance and agility on a rock-climbing wall, participants are encouraged to push themselves and embrace the thrill of adventure.
