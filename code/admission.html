Let There Be Light (LTBL) admission process prioritizes creating an inclusive and supportive environment for prospective students, ensuring their holistic development and success. The admission requirements at LTBL encompass several facets to ascertain the suitability and readiness of applicants to become part of the institute's vibrant educational community. Prospective students are typically required to complete an application form, providing essential personal details and educational background information. Additionally, applicants may need to submit academic transcripts or records from previous schools attended to assess their academic standing and readiness for the programs offered at LTBL.

Moreover, interviews or assessments might be conducted to gauge the student's aspirations, interests, and potential alignment with LTBL's values and educational philosophy. These interviews also offer an opportunity for prospective students and their families to learn more about the institute's culture, ethos, and educational offerings. Furthermore, the admission process may involve providing documentation of health records and immunizations to ensure the well-being and safety of all students within the LTBL community. Through a comprehensive and student-centered admission process, LTBL aims to foster a diverse and inclusive learning environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.
